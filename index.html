Hi! I am Qingqin Liu (刘晴钦 in Chinese). I am a PhD student of the MEI Lab at City University of Hong Kong, mentored by Prof. Kening Zhu. I received my Master’s degree in Communication from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Yanxiang Zhang. Prior to that, I completed a Bachelor of Science in Astronomy at USTC.

I am interested in enabling human-computer interactions using different modalities(touch, gestures, haptics, gaze, etc.), and on different devices (hand-held, head-mounted, in midair, esp. VR/AR/MR). Also, I am interested in game interaction and playable interfaces, game design and development, and player experience evaluation.
